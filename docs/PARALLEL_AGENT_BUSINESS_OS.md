# JOS Parallel Agent Business OS

## Verdict
JOS/CHEONOK is rebuilt as a governed multi-agent business operating system.
It runs five business lines in parallel under one orchestrator, one evidence ledger, and one Final Veto layer.

## Five Business Lines
1. PAPER Capital Intelligence
2. YouTube Channel Operation
3. AI Automation System Sales
4. AdSense / Affiliate Revenue
5. Web Subscription Service

## Architecture
- CEO Command Layer
- Central Orchestrator
- Five Business Squads
- Shared Service Agents
- Final Veto Layer
- Evidence / Revenue / Exit Ledger
